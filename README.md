# Fireblocks RegTech & Risk Report

Business analysis of **Fireblocks**—its emerging technologies (MPC custody and confidential computing), institutional user segments, **regulatory alignment** across EU/UK/SG/US/AU, and a **risk–response matrix**—with supporting visuals and a 5-minute investor presentation.

> Source: group report prepared for a Macquarie University FinTech unit. See `/report/Fireblocks_Report.pdf`. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

---

## What’s inside
- **Executive Summary & Company Overview** – what Fireblocks is and why it matters.
- **Emerging Tech** – MPC-CMP, TEEs (SGX/Nitro), chain connectivity, API-first controls.
- **Target Users & Value** – banks/fintechs, exchanges, asset managers, DeFi platforms.
- **Regulatory Environment** – EU MiCA, UK FCA (stablecoin custody & sandbox), MAS PSA, FinCEN CVC, AUSTRAC; **Global Compliance Map** and alignment table.
- **Key Business Risks & Responses** – likelihood × impact **risk matrix** and mitigations (operational, regulatory, market, third-party).
- **Slides** – 5-minute investor deck version of the report.
